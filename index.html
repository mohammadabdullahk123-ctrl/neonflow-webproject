Vickys Chippy
Located in the heart of Leicester, Vicky's Chippy has been serving up the city's finest fish and chips, alongside mouth-watering kebabs, since opening its doors. Whether you're craving crispy battered fish, golden chips, or a perfectly grilled kebab, we’ve got something to satisfy every appetite. With a focus on fresh ingredients, friendly service, and classic flavours, Vicky’s Chippy is the go-to spot for a quick bite or a hearty meal. Pop in for a delicious, no-frills takeaway experience that’s loved by locals and visitors alike!
